Thermostatic

Temperatur Control

Volume Control

Thermostatic valves have two handles, One controlling the water temperature and the other controlling the volume. Thermostatic valves react to temperature, not the pressure of the water (Hence the name thermostatic). With a thermostatic set up, you can change the flow volume without affecting the temperature, which allows for water conservation easy. This is possible due to a wax element inside the valve that expands / contracts in reaction to heat. The advantage with thermostatic is the direct control over the water output temperature.
