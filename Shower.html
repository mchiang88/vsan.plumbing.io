The Vision

Just bought your first home? Or maybe this has been your forever home and it needs a little bit of "updating". Either way, you pick up your phone(or tablet) and start Googling "Home remodel", "Interior Design", blah blah blah... . Everything looks so gorgeous online and you start visualizing on how to design your own home. There's the bathroom...oh wait...the kitchen as well...oh no what about the living room and bedrooms!

    Tips

  • Take a breath
  • Plan out one room at a time
  • Decide on a style (ie. Modern, Traditional, Transition(Inbetween the two)
  • Visit a showroom to touch and feel the products
  • Understand what sort of configurations you would like to go with / need to go with
  • Find a contractor that knows what your vision is
Okay, now that you're even more confused and the contractor has started to rip half your house out. We're here to help ;).
