Pressure Balance

Also known as Non-Thermostatic

Pressure balance / Non-Thermostatic features one handle on the trim controlling both volume and temperature. Water pressure is balanced via a mechanism (either a sliding disc on a piston, or a spool), that reacts to pressure change in the water pipes. Ex. Roommates flushing the toilet while you're showering. The pressure balance valves also provides antiscolding
